Overview

This Power BI report compares the performance of companies across various sectors, analyzing key financial metrics such as Return on Equity (ROE), profit margins, debt/equity ratios, and price/earnings ratios. It provides strategic insights for investment decisions and competitive analysis.

Key Features

  • Comparison of financial metrics (ROE, profit margins, debt/equity, price/earnings) across companies and sectors.
  • Interactive filters for symbols, countries, sectors, and industries.
  • Visualizations including scatter plots, bar charts, and histograms to identify trends and outliers.
  • Analysis of sector leverage and risk through debt/equity vs. beta scatter plots.

DAX Measures

Some key DAX measures used in the report:

Selected Companies Average ROE = AVERAGE('Companies'[ROE])
Selected Companies Average Profit Margin = AVERAGE('Companies'[Profit Margin])
Debt to Equity Ratio = DIVIDE(SUM('Companies'[Debt]), SUM('Companies'[Equity]), 0)

Power Query Transformations

Data preparation in Power Query included:

  • Filtering out incomplete records and standardizing company names.
  • Creating calculated columns for sector and industry categorization.
  • Normalizing financial metrics for consistent comparison across companies.

Results and Insights

The report provided actionable insights:

  • Companies in the Consumer Defensive sector (e.g., 22nd Century Group Inc) showed the highest debt/equity ratio (6.05), indicating higher financial risk.
  • Healthcare companies like 10x Genomics Inc had negative ROE (-25.17) and profit margins (-29%), suggesting operational challenges.
  • Consumer Cyclical companies like 1-800 Flowers.com Inc exhibited low leverage (0.60 debt/equity) and stable ROE (1.52).
  • Price/earnings ratios varied widely, with companies like Despegar.com and LiveRamp Holdings showing high ratios (over 100M), indicating potential overvaluation.
